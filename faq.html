Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SplitTheBill. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

Getting started is easy! You can begin using SplitTheBill in two ways:

  1. Guest Mode: Start immediately without creating an account. Your data will be stored locally in your browser.
  2. Create Account: Sign up for a free account to sync your data across devices and access advanced features.

Simply click "Get Started" on the homepage and choose your preferred option.

Guest Mode:

  • Start immediately without registration
  • Data stored locally in your browser
  • Data will be lost if you clear browser data or use a different device
  • Perfect for one-time use or testing

Account Mode:

  • Data synced across all your devices
  • Secure cloud backup
  • Real-time collaboration with other users
  • Access to advanced features and analytics

Creating and managing groups is simple:

  1. Go to the Dashboard and click "Create Group"
  2. Enter a group name and description
  3. Choose your default currency
  4. Add members by name and email (optional)
  5. Share the group link with others to invite them

You can edit group details, add/remove members, and manage permissions at any time.

Yes! SplitTheBill is designed to be inclusive:

  • You can add members by name only, even if they don't have accounts
  • Share a group link that allows guest access
  • Members can join in guest mode and upgrade to accounts later
  • All data will be preserved when they create accounts

SplitTheBill offers flexible splitting options:

  • Equal Split: Divide the expense equally among all members
  • Custom Amounts: Specify exact amounts for each person
  • Percentage Split: Assign percentages to each member
  • Share-based Split: Allocate based on shares (e.g., 2 shares for one person, 1 share for another)

You can also exclude specific members from an expense if needed.

SplitTheBill automatically calculates and tracks balances:

  • View individual balances for each group member
  • See who owes money and who is owed money
  • Get settlement suggestions to minimize transactions
  • Mark payments as settled when money is exchanged
  • Export detailed reports and summaries

Yes! You can attach receipt images to any expense:

  • Upload photos directly from your phone or computer
  • Supports common image formats (JPG, PNG, etc.)
  • Receipts are stored securely and can be viewed by group members
  • Include receipts in PDF exports for record-keeping

Upgrading is seamless and preserves all your data:

  1. Click "Create Account" in the guest mode warning or user menu
  2. Enter your email and create a password
  3. Verify your email address
  4. All your existing groups, members, and expenses will be automatically migrated

The process takes less than a minute and you won't lose any data.

Yes, we take data security and privacy seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use Firebase's enterprise-grade security infrastructure
  • Guest mode data stays on your device only
  • Account data is backed up securely in the cloud
  • We never share your personal information with third parties
  • You can export or delete your data at any time

SplitTheBill works on all modern browsers:

  • Desktop: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
  • Mobile: Chrome Mobile, Safari Mobile, Firefox Mobile
  • PWA Support: Can be installed as an app on most devices

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser.

Yes! SplitTheBill is designed to work offline:

  • View and edit existing data without internet connection
  • Add new expenses and members offline
  • Changes sync automatically when you're back online
  • Install as a PWA for native app-like experience

Note: Some features like real-time collaboration require an internet connection.

SplitTheBill can be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA):

On Desktop:

  1. Visit SplitTheBill in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox
  2. Look for the install icon in the address bar
  3. Click "Install" when prompted

On Mobile:

  1. Open SplitTheBill in your mobile browser
  2. Tap the share button (iOS) or menu (Android)
  3. Select "Add to Home Screen"
